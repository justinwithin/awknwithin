What to Expect

Your day at the ranch is yours to design. Here's how most guests spend their time.

Arrive & Settle In

Check in during open hours. Grab a towel, change, and take a few deep breaths. The land does the rest.

Reset Your Nervous System

Alternate between the dry sauna, cold plunge, and hot tub. Let your body find its rhythm through contrast therapy.

Gather Around the Fire

Join others at the fire pit for conversation, stillness, or just the warmth. Community happens naturally here.

Play & Move

Hit the pickleball court, explore the trails, or just stretch in the temple space. Move however your body wants to.

Rest & Restore

Find a hammock in the garden, lie back, and let the trees overhead remind you what slowing down feels like.

Leave Lighter

Most guests say they feel a palpable shift by the end of the day. That's not marketing — that's the land.
