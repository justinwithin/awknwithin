How It Works

1

Intake

Schedule a complementary consultation with our Admission team. We assess your medical history and current medications to confirm ketamine is an appropriate treatment.

2

Onboarding

We will schedule your onboarding call and medical consultation. We'll go over your medical history and schedule your first ketamine session.

3

Moving In

You will decide on a moving date with your Admission rep. We will send you a list of things to bring and how to prepare. Once you arrive, we will host a welcome ceremony and help you settle down.

4

Integration

Your therapist will create an after-care plan tailored specifically to you. You can continue treatment at Within with therapy and coaching add-ons.
